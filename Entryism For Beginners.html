Spreading culture is hard if you do it confrontationally. Gain entry, develop trust and gradually change from the inside.

So, you want to change the corporation to a Lean/Agile culture? But you know that your values and culture are significantly different from the corporation, and to do so openly will invite being resisted strongly. Good. Welcome to the problem that evangelical movements have faced forever. While you have to acknowledge the persistence of the Mormons and Jehova's Witnesses, or the people selling Socialist Worker, you have to admit that their success rate isn't great, not least because they attract strong opposition.
